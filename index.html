PointsCriteria

20 ptsCreativity: How innovative and DARING is your project?

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of problems should I tackle? I'm lost!

Try the UN Development Goals website for ideas. Think big, and think impactful.

What is the overall division? Can I win it?

If you sign up for 3 or more divisions, you're automatically entered. Your average score across your divisions will be your overall score.

I created a project this semester for class or a club or for fun, can I use that project?

Yes, absolutely!!! That's the idea, to show off cool things you've made!

Can I win more than one division/prize?

Unfortunately, no. You may only win one of the individual divisions or win the overall (which is what you want).
