2024 D128 STEAM Showcase

An opportunity for VHHS students to showcase their STEAM projects that they have worked so hard on this past semester. Students will compete in the categories: Science, Technology, Engineering, Entrepreneurship, Equity, Arts, and Math to create solutions to real-world problems.

Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Problem Identification (30 pts)
  • Solution (20 pts)
  • Creativity (20 pts)
  • Theme (20 pts)
  • Presentation (10 pts)

Project Examples

  • Science: Experiment on stress' contributions to tumor progression
  • Technology: Student-made video games
  • Engineering: Road redesigns of Route 60
  • Entrepreneur: Homework helper, stock trading program
  • Equity: Proposal for ending child marriage
  • Arts: Pending
  • Math: Pending
